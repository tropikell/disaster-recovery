What this app is

The Disaster Recovery Companion is a free, community-created tool designed to help Hawaiʻi residents organize and document their recovery after a natural disaster. It provides checklists, document templates, a photo log, a daily journal, and links to local resources.

This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any government agency, insurance company, or commercial organization.

No guarantees on information accuracy

Important disclaimer: While we have made every effort to provide accurate and helpful information, we cannot guarantee that all resources, links, guidance, or document templates are always current, complete, or applicable to your specific situation. Laws, policies, and available resources change frequently — especially in the aftermath of a disaster.

Always verify critical information directly with:

  • Your insurance company and adjuster
  • FEMA (disasterassistance.gov or 1-800-621-3362)
  • Hawaiʻi Emergency Management Agency (HIEMA)
  • Maui Emergency Management Agency (MEMA)
  • City and County of Honolulu Department of Emergency Management (DEM)
  • Hawaiʻi Insurance Division (1-844-808-DCCA)
  • A licensed attorney or public adjuster if you have legal or claims disputes
