Privacy Policy

How we handle your information

Last updated: March 2026
The short version: This app does not collect, store, or share your personal information with anyone. Everything you enter stays on your own device or in your own personal Google Drive account — fully under your control.

Who we are

The Disaster Recovery Companion is a free community tool created to help Hawaiʻi residents navigate the documentation and recovery process after a natural disaster. It is not operated by a company and does not have a commercial purpose.

What information the app uses

When you use this app, you may enter personal information such as your name, address, phone number, email address, insurance details, and damage descriptions. You may also upload photos of damage to your property.

This information is used solely to:

  • Auto-fill recovery documents (insurance emails, FEMA reports, contractor requests)
  • Save your progress locally on your device
  • Sync your data to your own personal Google Drive account if you choose to sign in
  • Auto-sync your journal entries to a Google Doc in your own Google Drive if you are signed in

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in two places only, both under your direct control:

  • Your device's browser storage (localStorage) — data stays on the device and browser you are using. It is never transmitted to any external server by this app.
  • Your personal Google Drive — if you choose to sign in with Google, your recovery data, photos, and journal are saved directly to your own Google Drive account. No one else can access your Drive files.

Google sign-in and permissions

If you choose to sign in with Google, this app requests the following permissions:

  • drive.file — to create and update files in your Google Drive for backup purposes. The app can only access files it creates — not any other files in your Drive.
  • userinfo.profile — to display your name after sign-in so you know which Google account is connected.
  • Google Docs API — to automatically sync your daily journal entries to a Google Doc in your Drive.

This app does not access, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive. Your Google account credentials are handled entirely by Google — this app never sees your password.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transmit your personal information to any third party — ever. Your data is yours.

The only external services this app communicates with are:

  • Google APIs — solely to enable Drive and Docs sync at your request
  • Google Fonts — to load the app's typefaces (no personal data is involved)

Photos

Photos you add to the Photo Log are stored temporarily in your browser session only. They are not uploaded anywhere unless you explicitly tap "Upload photos to Google Drive," at which point they are sent directly to your own Google Drive folder.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, you can reach out via the GitHub repository for this project.
